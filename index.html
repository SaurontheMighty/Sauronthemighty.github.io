Android App

I wanted to build a productivity app that could time me and store the amount of time I study each day. With graphs to show patterns and changes. Currently the app has three modes: stopwatch, pomodoro and timer.

I usually build tools that I need. My first productivity tool was called Ashish Life Solutions and it was a simple swift script that ran in the background and reminded me to take an eyebreak with a notification every 10 minutes. I only built it because at the time I couldn't find any free MacOS app that could do that. The main reason I built this app for Android because I have an Android phone.

The app has a modular design so that I can keep adding features as I get time.


Pygame

I made this game using pygame the python library. I wanted to make a game that was 100% mine and even drew the art myself on piskelapp. Only the music files aren't mine, all sources are listed in the code. This program is really well structured, I broke it into sections with a combination of comments and functions so that it's really easy to understand and debug. It has two stages, in the first one you have to avoid the obstacles and once you reach a score of 100 it takes you to a boss fight.
